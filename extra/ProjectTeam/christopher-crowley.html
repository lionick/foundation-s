Christopher B. Crowley, Ph.D

Christopher B. Crowley, Ph.D. is an Assistant Professor of Teacher Education at Wayne State University in Detroit, Michigan. His primary area of research is curriculum studies and he focuses on issues surrounding privatization in teacher education. He is a former English teacher, and he has more than two decades of experience as an educator.  Crowley’s research has appeared in journals such as Teaching and Teacher Education, Review of Research in Education, Teachers College Record, The China Quarterly, and Schools: Studies in Education.
