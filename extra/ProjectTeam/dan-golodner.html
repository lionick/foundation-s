Dan Golodner

Dan Golodner is the archivist for the American Federation of Teachers at the Walter P. Reuther Library since 1997. He has built the collection into one of the largest archives of teacher union materials in the country and has conducted oral histories, produced exhibits and given numerous talks about the American teacher union movement.
