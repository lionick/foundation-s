Min Yu

Min Yu is an Associate Professor in the College of Education at Wayne State University. She uses ethnography, along with archival research and policy analysis, to develop critical understandings of the roles of community organizing and the impact of changing social, political, and economic conditions on the education of children. Her work appears in journals such as The China Quarterly, Comparative Education Review, Review of Research in Education, Educational Studies, Curriculum Perspectives, Diaspora, Indigenous, and Minority Education.
