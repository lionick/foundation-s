Welcome to Bridging the Gap, a project aimed at creating partnerships between teachers and archivists to create engaging, place-based primary source lessons.

Here, You’ll Find Information About Our Experience Working Together As Well As Lessons Created By Teachers Working With Our Project Team.


Lessons Are Designed To Develop Historical Thinking Skills So That Your Students Can Explore Historical Documents And Develop A Rich Understanding Based On Context And The Interplay Between Different Perspectives. We Encourage You To Use This Tool In Your Own Omeka-S Projects To Help Illuminate Archival. Practices For A Broad Audience.
